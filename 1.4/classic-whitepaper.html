Whitepaper

As envisaged in the original Aventus whitepaper A Blockchain-Based Event Ticketing Protocol (2017), Aventus Classic is an Ethereum-based ticketing protocol designed to alleviate fraud and touting in the long tail of the event ticketing industry.

For more information; including a history of the design decisions taken here, see the updated Aventus Classic Whitepaper (2019)
